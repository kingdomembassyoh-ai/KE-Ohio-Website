Our Vision

We are a praying people, carrying a prophetic mandate.

Kingdom Embassy Ohio is a prophetic, Spirit-led church committed to hosting the presence of God and preparing believers for the return of the Lord Jesus Christ.

We gather to encounter God, grow in spiritual maturity, and be equipped to demonstrate His Kingdom in our families, cities, and the nations.

Our Mission

  • Ignite hearts through prayer, worship, and the Word.
  • Raise prophetic voices who discern and declare Heaven’s agenda.
  • Develop spiritual gifts and callings for Kingdom assignment.
  • Impact our region with the love, power, and compassion of Christ.

Plan Your Visit

We’re expecting you.

We want your visit to be simple, clear, and full of peace. Here’s what you can expect when you come.

What to Expect

Services are filled with passionate worship, prayer, and prophetic ministry. Come dressed comfortably—casual or dressy, you’ll fit right in.

  • Services last about 2–2.5 hours.
  • Live worship and prophetic flow.
  • Prayer and ministry available at the altar.

Families & Children

We love families and the next generation. Children are welcome in the main sanctuary and may have moments of focused prayer and ministry.

  • Kids can worship with parents.
  • Family-friendly environment.
  • Space for parents with little ones as needed.

Parking & Arrival

We are easy to find with GPS using the address below. Our team will gladly help you find a seat.

  • Arrive 10–15 minutes early.
  • Use “1783 Melrose Ave, Columbus, OH 43224” in your GPS.
  • Come with expectation—God is moving.

New Here?

Welcome home to Kingdom Embassy Ohio.

If you’re visiting for the first time, we want you to know you are not here by accident. We believe the Holy Spirit orders steps, and we are honored to walk with you.

Whether you’re hungry for more of God, seeking healing, or simply curious, there is room for you here.

  • No church background required—just come as you are.
  • We value presence over performance.
  • We believe in the gifts of the Spirit and the prophetic.
